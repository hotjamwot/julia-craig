Julia Craig

About

I'm a non-fiction writer based in London, with a particular interest in cultural criticism and personal essays. My work has appeared in various literary magazines and journals, exploring themes of identity, belonging, and the ways we navigate our increasingly complex world.

When I'm not writing, you'll find me wandering through galleries, reading in quiet cafés, or having long conversations about books and ideas. I believe in the power of the essay to capture fleeting moments of understanding and to make sense of our shared human experience.

My essays have been featured in Granta, The London Review of Books, Literary Review, and The White Review, amongst others. I'm currently working on my first collection of essays, exploring themes of memory, place, and belonging.
